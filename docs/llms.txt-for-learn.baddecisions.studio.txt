# Ultimate Unreal Engine Program — Bad Decisions Studio

> A premium step-by-step program teaching Unreal Engine 5 from beginner to portfolio-ready. Built by Bad Decisions Studio for creators, artists, and studios.

The Ultimate Unreal Engine Program is the flagship Unreal Engine education product from Bad Decisions Studio. It covers virtual production, MetaHuman characters, environment design at 4K, advanced lighting, and cinematic storytelling. 55 modules, 20+ hours of content, used by 1,500+ students across 80+ countries.

- Virtual production fundamentals

- Environment building at production-grade quality (4K)

- 55 modules · 20+ hours · self-paced

## About the studio

Bad Decisions Studio is a creative technology studio focused on AI, real-time 3D, and content. Run by Farhad Shababi and Faraz Shababi and a team from Vancouver and Dubai. The studio operates across three pillars: Content (podcast + videos), Education (premium programs and free tutorials), and Services (consulting, AI-enabled solutions, training, interactive experiences, content strategy).
