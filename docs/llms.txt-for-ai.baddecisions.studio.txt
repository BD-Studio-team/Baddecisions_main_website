# Ultimate AI for Creatives Program — Bad Decisions Studio

> A hands-on program teaching AI workflows, tools, and playbooks for creators, artists, and operators. Built by Bad Decisions Studio.

The Ultimate AI for Creatives Program is the flagship AI education product from Bad Decisions Studio. It teaches practical AI workflows, curated tool picks, and step-by-step playbooks designed to help creators save time, win clients, and build with modern AI tooling.

## Pages

- [Program homepage](https://ai.baddecisions.studio): Curriculum, modules, pricing, enrollment
- [Student Academy](https://academy.baddecisions.studio): LMS for enrolled students (login)

## What's covered

- Foundation: how modern AI works for creative work
- Toolbox: curated set of AI tools for image, video, audio, copy, and automation (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Midjourney, Runway, ElevenLabs, HeyGen, Kling, and others)
- Playbooks: step-by-step recipes for common creative jobs (brand work, video edits, ideation, automation)
- Workflows that combine AI with traditional creative tools (Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere Pro, Unreal Engine)

## About the studio

Bad Decisions Studio is a creative technology studio focused on AI, real-time 3D, and content. Run by Farhad Shababi and Faraz Shababi and a team from Vancouver and Dubai. The studio operates across three pillars: Content (podcast + videos), Education (premium programs and free tutorials), and Services (consulting, AI-enabled solutions, training, interactive experiences, content strategy).
