The Story

Two brothers.
One bad decision.

"We started with $0, no clients, and no followers. Just a camera, a computer, and a belief that creative technology should be accessible to everyone. That one bad decision to quit the comfort zone and build something from nothing — it was the best decision we ever made."

Bad Decisions Studio was built by Faraz (Commander) and Farhad Shababi — two brothers operating across Vancouver and Dubai. From zero to millions of students, partnerships with Epic Games, YouTube, and Snapchat, and client work for Dubai Police and A2RL.

Today BDS is a full creative technology brand: a podcast reaching millions, a course platform with students in 80+ countries, live events, and a suite of proprietary AI tools built to push the boundaries of what a modern studio can do.
